About Me

Hi there, I’m Deviano Dames, a creative and passionate individual originally from The Bahamas. I currently reside in London, Ontario, where I pursued my interest in Interactive Media Design at Fanshawe College. During my time there, I discovered my passion for design and coding, I am also an avid learner and enjoy keeping up with the latest trends and technologies in my field. I find inspiration in the world around me and Enjoy exploring new creative avenues whenever possible. When I’m not busy working on projects or learning new skills, I enjoy spending time Outdoors, trying new foods and spending time with family and friends. I believe that a positive attitude and a willingness to learn are the keys to Success, and I am excited to continue growing both personally and professionally in the years to come.
