Description:

At the start of the semester, I was given the task of creating a stadium, designing a team logo, and bringing it to life with a dynamic video introduction for a sports network.

Maxon Cinema 4D and After Effects

Problems and Solutions:

I faced challenges in this project, particularly with the cloth tag while attempting to achieve a dynamic effect, making the banner appear as if it was blowing in the wind. Despite various attempts, a solution remained elusive. I have intentions to revisit and address this issue, along with further enhancements planned for this project. Stay tuned for updates!
